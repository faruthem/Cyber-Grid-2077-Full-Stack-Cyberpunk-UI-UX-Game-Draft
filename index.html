Move your cursor through the network using W A S D or ↑ ← ↓ →. You are the cyan ▣ node.

02

HACK DATA NODES

Reach yellow ◆ data nodes to collect credits. Press SPACE or H to hack all adjacent nodes at once for double points.

03

EVADE TRACKERS

Fuchsia ◉ trackers hunt you across the grid. If one reaches your position, your connection is terminated. Use red ▓ firewalls as barriers - trackers cannot pass through them.

04

FIREWALL BREACH PROTOCOL

Hack all nodes surrounding a firewall ▓ to make it vulnerable ⊗ (yellow cross). Step onto a vulnerable firewall to destroy it, triggering an EMP that freezes trackers for 5s, earning +50 credits, and gaining a 3s data shield that blocks one tracker attack. Destroying adjacent firewalls creates chain reactions.

05

LEVEL UP

Accumulate credits to raise your clearance level. Higher levels spawn more data nodes but also more aggressive trackers. Survive as long as possible.
